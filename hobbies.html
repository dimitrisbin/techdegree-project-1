Hobbies

Sad Disco, my music project, has been around since 2013.

After many live performances and touring in Greece, came the release of a 7" vinyl single and video for the song "Heart", followed by the release of the debut self titled LP in 12" vinyl.

From the beginning of the first song "Beat", also featured in video, with its' dark rhythmical progress until the ninth song "Dawn" the optimistic album closer, presents a time traveling, almost vintage production, with very intense emotional songs and strong melodies.
